About Me

I am an Accelerator Physicist specializing in beam dynamics and machine learning applications in particle accelerators. With expertise in both theoretical physics and practical implementation, I develop innovative solutions for beam optimization and accelerator control systems.

My current work focuses on integrating machine learning with particle tracking simulations, developing sophisticated beam optimization algorithms, and implementing advanced diagnostic tools. I utilize TraceWin and CST Studio Suite for precise beam dynamics simulations and electromagnetic field analysis.

I have successfully developed several key applications, including an Orbit Response Matrix (ORM) analyzer and various beam diagnostic tools. My work combines classical accelerator physics principles with modern computational methods to enhance accelerator performance and reliability.
